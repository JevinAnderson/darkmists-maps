The Crusader Bar

Broken bottles, a twisted and mangled bar, charred stools: This was once
a place for Crusaders to come and relax and talk about battles, but there
is nowhere to relax in this mess now. You see no sign of the barkeep either.
Did he get away with some of his stock of drinks? Did he get away at all?
You see no sign of his having died here...
