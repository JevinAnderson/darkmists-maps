The Halls of Memory

Any sound created in this hall echoes down the large stretch of the stone
domed ceiling. The finely polished stones that make up the walls and floor
are scattered with works of art. Paintings display the tales of Crusader
immortals. Scenes of great battles between the Crusader gods and their divine
enemies jump out in vivid colors and detail. A feeling of awesome power
entirely foreign to mortal experience surges though many of the works of art.
Large marble statues representing the titans themselves line the long stone
hall. The mere act of traveling past the statues evokes an overwhelming sense
of awe. On the walls near the Inner Keep you see a number of haphazardly
spaced human handprints in dried blood.
