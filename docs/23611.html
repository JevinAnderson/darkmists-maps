Justicar barracks

The walls of this low room are lined with rows of bunkbeds, packed
tightly together. The dirty stone floor bears a number of worn
pieces of clothing, casually tossed aside by owners in a hurry to meet
their next obligation as lawmen. The bunks themselves, however, are
tightly and uniformly made- sheets tucked firmly beneath the mattress,
pillows on top. Some of the beds have small chests next to them for
personal effects, but they contain nothing of note. A wide, shallow
pit interrupts the floor southward, and the barracks continue to the
north.
