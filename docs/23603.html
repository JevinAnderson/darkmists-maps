Altar of Law

You have now arrived in the heart of the Citadel of Justice, the Altar of
Law.  Once considered sacred by the now defunct Enforcers, this room is
rumored to still house the legendary Book of Law.  A grand, ancient pillar
stands in the middle of this white marble room.  The room is a perfect square,
constructed from the purest of white marble.  A golden dome serves as the
roof.  Repleat with windows, the roof reflects the sunlight that pours in,
making this entire room glow with a divine lustre.
