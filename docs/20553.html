Within a Tent

Out from under the harsh glare of the sun, this tent feels cool and
comfortable. All about you there boxes and crates, vases the size of
halflings, and coffers overflowing with ungents and spices. It's a
merchants dream having so many rare commodities under one roof. The
air is stuffy in this tent; a mixture of sweat, dust, and a myriad of
spices and scents the likes you can not put your finger on.
