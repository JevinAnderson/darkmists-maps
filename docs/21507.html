Top Level in the Tower

Standing on the topmost level of the Hourglass tower, you feel slightly short
of breath.  The air appears significantly thinner.  Peering over the ledge of
the platform you are on, you can see that it's quite a long drop down.  Thank-
fully, surrounding the platform is a 5 foot high wall, preventing you from
slipping out into the air.  Up ahead to the east you can see a large circular
room.  You can head back down to the main foyer of the tower if you so choose.
