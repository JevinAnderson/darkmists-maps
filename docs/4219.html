A Very Unholy Place

Unholy runes adorn every inch of the charred and blackened walls of this
despicable cave. Pulses of negative energy from the nether planes erupt
from a column of black-orange lava that flows from ceiling-to-floor in
a haphazard fashion causing the swarms of insects crawling around the room
to almost dance and gyrate in a disturbing fashion. A dull hum emanates
from the lava pillar, setting your pulse racing and causing your breathing
to become shallow and difficult. Once in a while, flashes of red and orange
light burst out from the unholy runes, fueled with negative energies that
draw their origin from the center of the lava column. It would be unwise to
linger here long.
