Before the Justicar stronghold

The undergrowth on either side of the path to the north thins as it
approaches the glass-domed structure to the south. The building's high
walls of white marble thrust forth from the ground beneath, their well-
polished surface gleaming much like the iridescent path to the north.
Supported by thick pillars of granite at each corner of the building,
the wall frames a tall, pointed arch that allows passage to the south.
Within the arch hang thick wooden doors, inlaid with decorative runes of
shining platinum and softly glowing mithril. The arched gate appears open
but entrance into the structure may not be a good idea without the
permission of the Stronghold.
