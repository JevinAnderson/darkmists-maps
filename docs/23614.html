A Suspended Walkway

This walkway connects the northern and southern ends of the second
floor of the stronghold. Support beams of painted wood connect reach
across to the base of the domed ceiling and support the walkway. The
walkway itself is made of finely stained and laquered wood. A sturdy
wooden railing keeps you from falling over the edge to the foyer below.
You may walk to the stairwell at the southern end of the walkway or
you may walk northward into the Great Hall.
