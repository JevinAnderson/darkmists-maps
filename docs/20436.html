By the herb garden

The rock hard, soil-strewn path extends east and west here, with a branch off
heading southward. On all sides you see an herb garden, the herb plants blos-
soming nicely. Honey bees buzz about, and sometimes you see one sipping
nectar from a nearby herbal flower. At the far western end of the path you
see a neat looking wooden structure, its doorway open.
