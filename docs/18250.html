A bend in the path of polished stone

The stone beneath you is polished almost to a perfect shine. There are
few imperfections, if any at all, in the stone here. The rock wall forms
an overhanging ceiling above you, and you are protected from a straight
downfall of water. There is such a downfall of water to your east, but
remaining on the path of polished stone will prevent you being dashed to
the rocks below which sit in the clear blue spring of water. The path
continues to the east and to the south.
