Citadel of Justice: Main Foyer

The foyer is truly magnificent.  The room starts as a solid marble floor
which stretches a good distance into the heart of the Citadel.  Ironwood
columns erupt from this floor and reach the arched ceiling forming
butresses at the top.  Exits appear to be in all cardinal directions.
Menacing looking guards must surely have roamed these halls once.  However,
the citadel appears now largely abandoned, though you think you can make out
fresh footprints in the dust on the floor.  Up to the north, you see a radiant
room.  The south will take you into the courtyard of the Citadel, and to the
East and West appear to be long hallways.
