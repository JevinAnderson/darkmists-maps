The Northwest Turret

The turret is simple in adornment, and meant to serve a specific purpose.
it grants a view of the grounds around the castle, so that intruders can
be watched for.  This turret overlooks the forests to the North, as well
as the path to the Grey Havens to the West.  The fine marble of the rest
of the place is not to be found here.  Only the soft blue carpets still
look like the rest.  The stairs lead back down to the castle proper,
and are also covered in the fine blue carpetting.
