Enforcer Barracks

This is the area where agents of Enforcer once rested themselves after a
weary day of protecting Theran citizens.  Simple and austere, the barracks
are little more than bunk beds arranged as efficiently as possible down the
long rectangular room.  Wooden closets stand beside each set of beds providing
storage of personal items. You notice these cabinets have no locks.  A
testament to the trust these agents of law must have had for each other.
