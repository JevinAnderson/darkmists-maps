Floating in Nothingness

There is truly nothing your mind can detect in any shape or form as far as
the senses reveal in any direction.  The crackling portal that led you here
is gone, and the only thing providing you with any guidance whatsoever is
a strong surge of power coming from above.  A mass of congealed globulous
slimy water gathers beneath you.  How it got here and its purpose is not
easily ascertained.
