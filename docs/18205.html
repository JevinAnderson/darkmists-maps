Along the garden path

Northward and southward the path of natural stone stretches. To either
side of it, high, thick trees surrounded by lush green shrubs send
shade from their thick canopy of leaves above. To the east is a
running stream of water heading toward the north. Several low-hanging
branches promise trouble to those who do not duck low to pass beneath
them. A lone leaf flutters downward and lands upon the rushing waters,
floating swiftly northward like a flatbottom boat.
