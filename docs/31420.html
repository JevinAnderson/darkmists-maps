Inside the Vledimire Memorial

There is a sense of peace and holiness about this building. The
pristine white marble outside is nothing compared to the marblesque
walls and floor within. They practically glow with a holy aura.
On the north wall is a portrait of Vledimire. A framed scroll hangs
upon the eastern wall. In the middle of the room is a pedestal
bearing a white book.
