Council Room

Like many rooms in the Citadel of Justice, this room is very simple and
elegantly decorated.  The council room is a large trapezoid shape with the
coats of arms of past Enforcer leaders lining the walls.  A gigantic table
sits in the center of the room with a large chair at the shorter of the two
parallel sides, awaiting the leader of enforcers.  Smaller chairs align the
other sides of the table, awaiting other special agents of Justice.
