On a dark path

A dark canopy of green leaves arcs over the hard path beneath you. To the
east are woods, growing thickly, unhindered. The soil-strewn path stretches
northward and southward, with a fork heading toward the west. Little plants
line the path, their light green and bright yellow blooms lending a little
color to the darkness. Berry-bearing shrubs grow beyond the little flowering
plants, their leaves lush and dark green. You near the buzzing of a honey bee
or two, but only rarely do you see the little insect sipping nectar from one
of the pretty yellow blossoms.
